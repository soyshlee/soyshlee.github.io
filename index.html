About Me

If you clicked the "about me" tab then my sticky-notes must not have satiated your curious wonder. Well... if you haven't guessed already, this portfolio is based on the Office (not the UK version, never the UK version).
Why? Because I wanted my portfolio to showcase who I am and since the Office built my sense of humor and love for mockumentaries, I decided it would be the best theme to go with!
